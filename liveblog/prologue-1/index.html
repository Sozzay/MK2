Prologue - Part 1

PROLOGUE
That day wasn’t just another regular day for me.
No... it was more nerve-wracking than I could have ever anticipated.
PROLOGUE
The reality of the situation didn’t set in until I was actually standing in front of the building.
Adrenaline began to rush through me, as if I was committing a horrible crime.
Something... completely unlike transferring to a new school.
PROLOGUE
Then again, people have always told me this school is far from ordinary.
In the same way little league players want to one day score big, students across the country dream about enrolling here.
Enrolling... at Hope’s Peak Academy.
To be honest, I find the fanfare a little weird.
As prestigious as the school may be, you shouldn’t ‘worship’ what is, at the end of the day, still a business.
PROLOGUE
Hope's Peak Academy is a government-funded school for the privileged elite of society.
It recruits top students from every imaginable field to inspire hope, and in doing so, shape the future.
When people talk about Hope’s Peak Academy, they always say the same thing:
“If you come here and manage to graduate, you’ll be set for life.”
That may sound like an exaggeration, but many of the current industry leaders are actually graduates of Hope’s Peak.
For that reason, people have come to refer to the school by another name: “The Academy of Hope”.
PROLOGUE
There are two requirements for becoming a student at this school...
“One, you must be a current high school student.”
“Two, you must be the very best at what you do.”
Unlike other schools, Hope’s Peak Academy doesn’t hold entrance exams. The only way to get in is if the school scouts you itself.
If you accept their invitation and enroll, you’ll be recognised as an “Ultimate” something or other.
Ultimate Swimmer, Ultimate Tennis Pro... It depends on the field of work you’re good at.
To be frank, I’ve always thought of myself as an ordinary student, maybe a high achiever if I want to be generous...
...but that all changed when I got an invitation from Hope’s Peak.
Before I go any further, I guess I should introduce myself.
PROLOGUE
Um, my name is Mahiru Koizumi.
I’m the... Ultimate Photographer.
Calling myself that feels a little silly, but my mom seriously insists on the name.
Like I was saying, I received an invitation a few months back.
Naturally it came as a huge surprise.... I was no “prodigy", just a normal kid with a passion for photography.
Even so, I accepted Hope’s Peak’s offer.
I mean, it is Hope’s Peak. Even if I find its following a little weird, attending pretty much guarantees success in life.
Turning down an opportunity like that... would be stupid.
PROLOGUE
Of course, the road ahead won’t be an easy one.
Will I be able to overcome its hurdles? Can I do the academy proud? Can I do my family proud?
If I try my best, I’m sure it’ll be okay.
PROLOGUE
It’ll be okay...
PROLOGUE
It’ll be okay...
PROLOGUE

PROLOGUE

PROLOGUE
...It’s a door...
...Why is there a door here?
...Maybe... I should go inside?
No, I must go inside.
...“Must”?
What? When did I decide that?
No... I have to go in.
I must... go in.
PROLOGUE

PROLOGUE

O-Oh... uh...

I see...
I shouldn’t have barged into the room like that... Now everyone’s eyes were turned on me...
Are these people... my new classmates?
Then... I’m inside Hope's Peak Academy? But how...?
If you’re confused, I assume freshmen like us have been gathered in this classroom.
Eh? We’re being gathered?
PROLOGUE

I was just about to do that...
Trying to hide my annoyance, I quickly closed the door behind me.
Anyway... what just happened?
PROLOGUE

And as if in a trance... I opened it and wound up here.
PROLOGUE

PROLOGUE

PROLOGUE

PROLOGUE

PROLOGUE
